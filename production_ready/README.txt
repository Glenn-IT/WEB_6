============================================================
  PRODUCTION READY FILES — touchandcarespa.online
============================================================

This folder contains files that are READY TO UPLOAD to InfinityFree.
Do NOT use these locally — they will break your localhost setup.

FILES IN THIS FOLDER:
  .htaccess   → Upload this to ROOT of your InfinityFree htdocs/
  .env        → Upload this to ROOT of your InfinityFree htdocs/
                ⚠️  FILL IN the database credentials first! (see .env)

HOW TO USE:
  1. Open .env in this folder
  2. Fill in your InfinityFree MySQL credentials (DBUSER, DBPWD, DBHOST, DBNAME)
  3. Upload BOTH files via FTP to the root folder of your InfinityFree site
  4. These will REPLACE the .htaccess you already have locally

REMINDER:
  - Your localhost files are untouched
  - These files only go on the LIVE SERVER
